# Stats

## Overview
Detailed analytics and visualization page for training data.

## Sections

### Time Range Selector
- Preset buttons: 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, 1 year, All
- Custom date range picker
- "This week vs last week" comparison toggle

### Summary Cards
- Total sessions
- Total volume
- Average session duration
- Training frequency (sessions/week)

### Volume Chart
Line/bar chart showing:
- Total volume per day/week
- Can filter by muscle group
- Hover to see details

### Muscle Group Distribution
Pie/donut chart showing:
- Volume percentage per muscle group
- Click to filter sessions by muscle

### Frequency Heatmap
Calendar heatmap showing:
- Training days intensity
- Green (heavy) to gray (rest)
- Shows consistency over time

### Movement Progress
Table/cards showing:
- Each movement trained
- Best performance (weight/reps)
- Progress trend (↑ improving, ↓ declining, → stable)

### Personal Records Section
List of PRs broken down by:
- Date achieved
- Movement
- Value (weight or reps)
- Compare to previous PR

### Streaks
- Current streak (consecutive weeks with training)
- Longest streak
- Rest day analysis

### Export
- "Export to Excel" button
- Date range selection for export
